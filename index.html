FAQ

Questions serious clients usually ask

Does VaultGuard AI require private keys?

No. It is positioned as a monitoring and alerting layer for treasury wallets. Clients should never need to share private keys for the core service.

What kind of risks does it help detect?

Large outbound transfers, suspicious wallet interactions, treasury anomalies, repeated small drains, and treasury blind spots across monitored wallets.

Who is this best for?

DAOs, Web3 startups, crypto treasury teams, and small-to-mid protocols that want faster visibility into wallet risk without building internal monitoring from scratch.
